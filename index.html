Tradeoffs across all 5 screens
AI transparency: All AI-generated content (summaries, risk scores, suggestions) must be clearly labeled. Teachers need to trust and override the AI.
Student privacy: COPPA, FERPA, and GDPR constraints limit what data can be collected from minors. Engagement AI must be opt-in with parental consent.
Performance overhead: Real-time AI features (heatmaps, attention scoring) require low-latency ML inference. Poor performance → teacher abandonment.
Onboarding cliff: Power features risk overwhelming new teachers. Progressive disclosure and default-off AI features are essential for adoption.
